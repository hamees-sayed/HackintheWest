AlgoSwarm

Welcome to AlgoSwarm.
AlgoSwarm is a unique opportunity for students to gain real-world experience in algorithmic trading, and learn from industry experts hosted by QuantHive. (Psst! Don't worry we done assume any pre-requisite knowledge of Finance.)

It's a two phase Algorithmic Trading competition hosted in a 24hrs hackathon format through an online mode. There will also be workshops with industry professionals to upskill students with all the knowledge required to participate in this Hackathon.


The first phase will be a no-code round where students will gain understanding about the structure and working of an Algorithmic Trading strategy and develop one using the no-code platform.

The qualified teams (grading based on a minimum threshold for both risk and performance metrics of the strategy) will proceed to round 2 which will have the option of Python based coding (along with no-code) for developing an algorithmic trading strategy and backtesting the same.



Whether you are a rookie or a veteran, it's time for a challenge!
